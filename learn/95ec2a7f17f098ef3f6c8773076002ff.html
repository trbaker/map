Map Activity 116

Middle School Map Activity | 15–20 minutes | ArcGIS Item ID: 95ec2a7f17f098ef3f6c8773076002ff

Big Question

What patterns can we discover by exploring a map about map Activity 116?

Teacher note: Provide students access to the ArcGIS map or layer before beginning.

Student Directions

  1. Open the assigned map or layer provided by your teacher.
  2. Zoom out, zoom in, and pan to explore the whole map.
  3. Click at least three map features or locations to read pop-ups or details.
  4. Use evidence from the map to answer the short-answer questions below.
  5. Be ready to share one pattern and one question you still have.

Map Questions

  1. What does this map show about map Activity 116?
  2. Where do you notice the clearest pattern, cluster, or contrast?
  3. Choose one specific place on the map. What evidence do you observe there?
  4. What might explain the pattern you identified?
  5. What additional data layer would help you better understand this map?

Exit Ticket

Claim: One important pattern I noticed is...

Evidence: I know this because the map shows...

Question: One thing I still wonder is...

Created for classroom use. Students should support answers with map evidence.
