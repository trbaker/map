Where are the world’s biomes and why are they there (Guided Lesson)

Middle School Map Activity | 15–20 minutes | ArcGIS Item ID: 68bb4ffab037408baaad808f20ed3eee

Big Question

What patterns can we discover by exploring a map about global biome locations and climate connections?

Teacher note: Provide students access to the ArcGIS map or layer before beginning.

Student Directions

  1. Open the assigned map or layer provided by your teacher.
  2. Zoom out, zoom in, and pan to explore the whole map.
  3. Click at least three map features or locations to read pop-ups or details.
  4. Use evidence from the map to answer the short-answer questions below.
  5. Be ready to share one pattern and one question you still have.

Map Questions

  1. What does this map show about global biome locations and climate connections?
  2. Where do you notice the strongest cluster, contrast, or pattern?
  3. Choose one location on the map. What evidence can you observe there?
  4. What might explain the pattern you found?
  5. What additional map layer or data would help you learn more?

Exit Ticket

Claim: One important pattern I noticed is...

Evidence: I know this because the map shows...

Question: One thing I still wonder is...

Created for classroom use. Students should support answers with map evidence.
